The Copium Wars

In the mystical realm of Aeternum, where death is but a fleeting concept, whispers abound of a legendary crystal forged in the crucible of ancient times. This enigmatic artifact, steeped in the magic of rewriting history, possesses the extraordinary ability to reshape the outcome of any lost battle, offering its wielder an illusory triumph. Tales of the crystal's power have ignited the ambitions of warriors and factions alike, drawing them into a relentless quest for dominance in the ever-shifting tides of war known as the Copium Wars.
